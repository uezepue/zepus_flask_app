About ZepusClinics

Who We Are

ZepusClinics is a forward-thinking digital healthcare platform committed to bridging the gap between patients and healthcare professionals. We offer smart triage tools powered by AI, seamless referrals to public or private specialists, and a user-friendly environment for consultations — online, in person, or at home.

Our Mission

To make healthcare accessible, affordable, and efficient by integrating intelligent technology with compassionate care.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every individual — regardless of location — can access expert medical guidance and care through the power of digital innovation.

Our Team

Founded by medical professionals and powered by technologists, our team is passionate about transforming how healthcare is delivered and experienced in Nigeria and beyond.
